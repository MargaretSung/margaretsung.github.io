Which two websites are you most inspired by?

1.squarespace.com
2.jolinelin.com

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Both sites use a ton of visual imagery and symmetry. The copy is simple and straightforward for the reader. The negative space frames the focal points and creates a call to action to choose a template or click on a photo. The copy is minimal which enhances the visual imagery.

What makes them similar and different?

They both use negative space to frame their focal points and they both use strong imagery to tell a story. Both are artistic and have minimal copy. The contrast is demonstrated through the visual cues and call to action. Squarespace is an interactive site where a user explores and envisions how they might use a template personally. Jolene Lin uses her artistry to create eyecandy and allows the user to explore freely without the goal of searching for a personal outcome.
